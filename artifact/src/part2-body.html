What this is

An independent, evidence-audited satirical documentation project about one operator's recorded experience across Anthropic's product surfaces. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by Anthropic. Product names are used for identification and commentary.

Method

Frontstage copy is satire and framing. Every substantive factual claim carries an evidence classification; analysis is always labeled as analysis; surfaces without evidence make no claims. Census and case data are a dated snapshot of 2026-07-18.

Sources

The sanitized session transcript (primary), the operator's pasted product-settings observations, live product documentation checks recorded in-session, and a de-identified research audit of the operator's corpus. The evidence repository: yaw-sh/The-Anthropic-Experience.

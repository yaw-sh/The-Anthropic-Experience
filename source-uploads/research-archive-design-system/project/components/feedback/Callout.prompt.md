Bordered note block with a colored left accent bar — the archive's fidelity notes and critical warnings.

```jsx
<Callout variant="info" title="Fidelity + redaction note:">
  Reconstructed by the assistant from in-session context…
</Callout>

<Callout variant="alert">Even public repos blocked.</Callout>
```

`variant`: info (blue) · alert (red). `title` is the bold lead line; `icon` overrides the default glyph.

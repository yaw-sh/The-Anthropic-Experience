## Fidelity and redaction

All private identifiers and sensitive reference slots are replaced before generalization with `###-PII-###` or a neutral description. Repetitive adjacent exchanges may be collapsed, but their source ranges and material outcomes remain in phase receipts. No generalized language is presented as a quotation.
